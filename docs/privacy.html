Stay Sharp — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

Stay Sharp ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how our iOS app handles your information.

Information We Collect

Stay Sharp does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not have accounts, sign-ins, or user profiles.

Microphone Access

Stay Sharp requests access to your device's microphone solely to detect the pitch of your guitar strings in real time. Audio is processed entirely on your device and is never recorded, stored, or sent anywhere.

Data Storage

All app data (such as your selected tuning or metronome tempo) is stored locally on your device only. We have no servers and collect no data.

Third-Party Services

Stay Sharp does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Stay Sharp does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no user-generated content, accounts, or data collection of any kind.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: staysharpapp@gmail.com
